Vendure Ecommerce

An ecommerce application based on vendure

Project Overview

Vendure is A modern, open-source headless commerce framework built with TypeScript & Nodejs. It is a new e-commerce framework built for the developers who are building the modern web.

It is also a headless commerce framework, meaning that it delivers content through its GraphQL API, leaving you free to implement your store-front applications in the technologies of your choice. Tailor your application by composing best-in-class services and APIs, with Vendure at the core.

While vendure comes with a preexisting set of features, I have been, for the past year, adding customizations according to customer requirements. Some of these include Customer and Customer Group based Discounts, Live Chat integrated with the store front, Stock,Sales and Refund Report, Store Front Conetnt Management System and much more.
